Annie's Vegan Mac

Ingredients

  • Box of Annie's Vegan Mac
  • Almond Milk
  • Butter
  • Nutritional Yeast
  • Broccoli

Steps

  1. Make box of mac per box instructions.
  2. While pasta is cooking, saute broccoli florets over medium heat until lightly crispy.
  3. When pasta is finished cooking, strain pasta and return to pot. Combine butter, almond milk, and packet ingredients in pot and stir.
  4. Add nutritional yeast and broccoli and stir until well combined.
