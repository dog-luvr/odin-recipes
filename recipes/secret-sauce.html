Secret Sauce

Ingredients

  • 1/4 Cup Soy Sauce
  • 1 tbspn Chinese Black Vinegar
  • 1 tbspn Rice Vinegar
  • 1/4 cup Sesame Oil
  • 1/4 cup Vegetable Oil
  • 1 large clove Garlic
  • 1 knob Ginger
  • 1 tbspn Szechuan Peppercorns
  • 3-4 Dried Chili Peppers
  • 1 tbspn Chili Crisp

Steps

  1. Chop one large clove of garlic and one knob of ginger. Combine in mixing bowl and set aside.
  2. Heat 1/4 cup of vegetable oil in small skillet. When hot, add a table spoon of szechuan peppercorns and 3-4 chili peppers. Turn heat off and let sit fo at least 5 minutes.
  3. Combine remaining ingredients into bowl with ginger and garlic. Add oil from peppercorns and chili peppers. Whisk to combine.
  4. Add to any dish or put in jar for later use.
